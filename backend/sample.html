Lesson 05

Financial Reality

⏱️ 3 min read
💰

Initial Investment Breakdown

  • Inventory ₹1,000–₹5,000
  • Cleaning & Preparation ₹200–₹500
  • Packaging Material ₹300–₹1,000
  • Photo Setup (Optional) ₹0–₹1,000
Minimum practical start: ₹1,500–₹3,000
📊

Unit Economics

  • Sourcing Cost ₹100–₹300/item
  • Selling Price ₹300–₹900/item
  • Extra Costs ₹50–₹150/item
  • Profit Per Sale ₹150–₹500
Typical margin: 40–70%
⏳

Break-even Reality

  • Sales Needed 10–20 sales
  • Strong Execution 3–6 weeks
  • Average Execution 2–3 months
Depends on sourcing quality, pricing & trust building.
🔁

Monthly Operating Costs

  • Inventory ₹3k–₹10k
  • Packaging ₹500–₹2k
  • Marketing/tools ₹0–₹2k
Running cost: ₹2,000–₹5,000/month
📈

Realistic Income Timeline

MONTH 1

₹0–₹5k

Build trust, upload products, get first buyers.

MONTH 2-3

₹5k–₹15k

Consistent posting and repeat customers.

6+ MONTHS

₹20k+

Scale suppliers, inventory and branding.

⚠️

Financial Risks

  • Buying products without understanding demand
  • Too much money stuck in inventory
  • Poor product photos reducing sales
  • Pricing too high before building trust
  • Slow customer acquisition
Start with 10–20 products, validate demand, then reinvest profits.
