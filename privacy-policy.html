Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 29, 2025 Last Updated: May 29, 2025

At Aptova, your privacy is at the heart of everything we do. This Privacy Policy describes how Aptova ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects information about you when you visit or use our platform at www.aptova.in. We encourage you to read this document in full. If you have any questions, our team is always happy to help at privacy@aptova.in.

01
Information We Collect

We only collect information that helps us deliver a better, more personalised experience for you.

Information You Provide

When you register or begin an assessment on Aptova, you may share:

  • Your name and email address, used to create and identify your account
  • A password of your choosing, stored using industry-standard encryption — it is never stored in a readable form and no one at Aptova can see it
  • Your responses during the assessment interview, such as details about your background, interests, skills, financial context, and goals — all of which help us tailor the experience to you
  • Your session history, so you can pick up exactly where you left off
Information We Collect Automatically

To keep the Platform running smoothly and continuously improve your experience, we collect certain technical information when you visit:

  • General location data at the city or region level, derived from your IP address
  • Your browser type, operating system, and device category
  • The pages you visit and how long you spend on them
  • Diagnostic and error information generated during your session

We use this information solely to improve the Platform — not to identify you personally.

Guest Sessions

If you prefer to explore Aptova without creating an account, that is completely fine. We create a temporary session to power your experience, but it is not linked to any personal identity. Guest session data is automatically and permanently removed after 30 days.

02
How We Use Your Information

We believe your data should work for you — not for us. Here is exactly how we use what you share:

  • To power your personalised assessment and deliver your business insights and report
  • To save your progress so registered users can return and continue at any time
  • To refine and improve the quality of questions, recommendations, and overall Platform experience over time
  • To send you essential account communications, such as password reset links or important service updates
  • To maintain the security and reliability of our systems
  • To fulfil any obligations required of us under applicable law

We do not use your information to serve advertisements, and we do not share or sell your data to any third party for commercial purposes.

As Aptova grows, we may introduce new features, services, or a revenue model in the future. Should any such development affect the way we collect or use your information, we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly and notify you in advance. Any future commercial features will be introduced transparently, and you will always have a clear choice about whether to participate.

03
AI Processing and How Your Answers Are Handled

Aptova's intelligent interview engine is powered in part by the OpenAI API. When you provide an answer, it may be processed through OpenAI's systems to determine the best follow-up question and to extract key information from what you have shared.

  • Under our agreement with OpenAI, your data is not used to train or improve their models
  • The meaningful information extracted from your responses — such as your interests, skills, and financial context — is stored securely within Aptova's own systems, kept separate from your raw answer text
  • OpenAI acts as a data processor on our behalf and is subject to strict data handling standards

For a full picture of OpenAI's practices, we recommend visiting openai.com/privacy.

04
How We Keep Your Data Safe

We have put thoughtful security measures in place to protect your information:

  • Your password is transformed using a one-way hashing process the moment you create it — it cannot be reversed or read by anyone
  • Every connection between your device and our Platform is protected by HTTPS encryption
  • Access to our database systems is restricted to authorised personnel only, under strict access controls
  • Your login sessions are protected by secure token management to prevent unauthorised access

We continuously review and strengthen our security practices. In the unlikely event of a security concern affecting your account, please reach out to us immediately at security@aptova.in.

05
How Long We Keep Your Data

We hold your data only for as long as it serves a genuine purpose:

Data Type How Long We Keep It
Registered account and assessment historyUntil you choose to delete your account
Guest session dataAutomatically deleted after 30 days
Diagnostic and AI interaction logsDeleted after 90 days
Anonymised platform usage statisticsRetained to improve the service (no personal identifiers)

You are always in control. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

06
When We Share Information

We treat your information with discretion. The only circumstances in which we share data are:

  • With OpenAI, strictly to process your responses as part of the assessment engine, as described in Section 3
  • With our infrastructure partners, who host and operate the Platform on our behalf under binding data processing agreements
  • When required by law, such as in response to a valid court order or legal obligation — and where permitted, we will notify you if this occurs
  • In a business transition, such as a merger or acquisition, where your data may transfer to a successor organisation. We will give you advance notice and the opportunity to delete your account before any such transfer
07
Your Rights Over Your Data

Your information belongs to you, and we make it straightforward to exercise your rights. You can contact us at privacy@aptova.in to:

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct any information that is inaccurate or out of date
  • Delete your account and all associated personal data from our systems
  • Export your assessment history in a structured, portable format
  • Withdraw from the Platform at any time — simply stop using it, and we will handle any outstanding data in line with our retention policy

We aim to respond to all requests within 30 days.

08
Cookies and Browser Storage

Aptova uses a small number of cookies and browser storage tools to make the experience work properly:

  • Session cookies keep you logged in as you move through the Platform
  • Functional storage preserves your progress if you are using a guest session
  • Analytics tools help us understand how the Platform is being used, using aggregated and non-identifiable data

You can adjust or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Please be aware that some features, particularly staying logged in, may not function as expected if cookies are turned off.

09
Children and Young People

Aptova is designed for individuals aged 16 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone younger than 16. If you believe someone under 16 has used the Platform and provided personal information, please let us know at privacy@aptova.in and we will remove the data promptly.

10
Future Revenue Model

Aptova is currently offered as a free platform. As we continue to grow and develop, we may introduce premium features, subscription plans, or other commercial offerings in the future. Should any such development affect the way we collect or use your information, we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly and notify you well in advance. Any future commercial features will be introduced transparently, and you will always have a clear choice about whether to participate.

11
Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as our Platform evolves. Whenever we make meaningful changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page and notify registered users by email or through an in-app message. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continuing to use Aptova after an update means you are comfortable with the revised policy.
