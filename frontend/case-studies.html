Why we built this
"Three students, one problem. No one could tell us which opportunity actually made sense for us."

We watched batchmates start side-hustles based on what was trending — and struggle because the business didn't match their actual situation. Some had capital but no time. Some had skills but no market. Some had market but no money. Aptova was built to answer the question none of our seniors could answer properly: what fits you, specifically?

Food & Nourishment
Priya, 22 — Vijayawada
B.Com final year · ₹3L available · Family has kitchen space
"I always wanted to do something with food. Everyone said open a restaurant. Aptova said cloud kitchen. That's a huge difference."
Priya had food passion, her family's spare kitchen space, and ₹3L. A restaurant franchise would have needed ₹15–20L and full-time presence. Aptova matched her to a cloud kitchen with a Strong Fit verdict — she had the exact setup it needed. She's now listed on Zomato with a tiffin and snack menu, running from home.

Technology Assistance
Rohan, 24 — Nagpur
Engineering grad · ₹0 capital · Weekends only available
"I thought I needed to build an app or freelance for clients abroad. Aptova showed me I was sitting next to a goldmine I kept ignoring."
Rohan had strong tech skills but was job-hunting and couldn't commit full-time. He lived in an area with a large retired population. Aptova flagged tech support for seniors as a high-fit opportunity — ₹0 to start, weekends only, genuine local demand. He charges ₹300/session and has 14 regular clients in his apartment complex alone.

Nature farming
Meena, 23 — Warangal
BA graduate · Family has 3 acres · ₹1.5L savings
"My family kept saying 'use the land' but nobody had a specific answer. Aptova gave me one in 10 minutes."
Meena had land, capital, and full-time availability — but no idea which opportunity to take. The validation stage revealed that mushroom cultivation was a Strong Fit: small space needed, quick returns, and she had family support for operations. Terrace farming kits got a Moderate Fit.
