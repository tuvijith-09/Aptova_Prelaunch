Common questions

Answers to what
you're probably thinking

Is Aptova free to use? +
Your first assessment is available at no cost — no credit card required. Because every report is powered by AI and uses real computing resources, we apply a fair-usage limit to keep the platform running. Most users will never hit that cap. If you need additional assessments beyond the limit, we'll offer affordable options — but getting started costs you nothing.
How many business opportunities does Aptova actually cover? +
135+ at launch across 11 opportunity domains — technology, campus, services, creative, commerce, health, eco, agriculture, food, education, and wildcard ideas. We add new opportunities every month based on what users tell us they need.
Do I need to create an account? +
Yes — a free account is required to start your assessment. Sign-up takes under 2 minutes and needs your name, email, and phone number. Your phone number is used to verify your account via OTP and for secure password resets — we don't use it for marketing. This also lets us save your results so you can revisit them anytime.
How accurate are the recommendations? +
The quality of your results depends on how honestly you answer. Aptova uses a structured scoring system — not random AI guesses. The more accurate your inputs, the more reliable the output. We also show the reasons behind every recommendation so you can judge for yourself.
What if I have no money to invest? +
Aptova covers many opportunities that start with ₹0. Tourist guiding, tech support for seniors, vernacular content creation, hyperlocal delivery — these need skills and time, not capital. Aptova will surface these for you if your profile fits.
Can I suggest an opportunity type that isn't covered yet? +
Yes — and we built a dedicated space for this. On the Explore Opportunities page, you'll find a "Suggest an Opportunity" button that opens a short submission form. Every suggestion goes to our admin team for review. Approved ideas are added to the live list in the next update. It's your platform — help us make it more complete.
When is Aptova launching? +
We're targeting mid-2026. Waitlist members get early access before the public launch, and their feedback directly shapes what we build and fix in the first few weeks.
Will you share or sell my data? +
Never. Your answers are used only to generate your report. We're students — we have no business interest in doing anything with your data and no infrastructure to monetise it even if we wanted to.
