Why we built this

"Three students, one problem. No one could tell us which opportunity actually made sense for us."

We watched batchmates start side-hustles based on what was trending — and struggle because the opportunity didn't match their actual situation. Some had capital but no time. Some had skills but no market. Some had market but no money. Aptova was built to answer the question none of our seniors could answer properly: what fits you, specifically?

Our Mission

We built Aptova to strip away the guesswork and give you an honest, mathematical validation of what you can actually do with the resources you have today.
